About Us

We live in an interconnected world where millions of devices generate and store unprecedented amounts of information every day. Properly processing this data, extracting knowledge, and learning from it is vital for optimal decision-making in various environments.

Our group consists of national and international researchers who are experts in the analysis, treatment, and extraction of knowledge from data.
